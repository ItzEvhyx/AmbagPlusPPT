§ 01

The Problem

Many people care about social and environmental issues but struggle to find a clear way to get involved and stay consistent. Without the right support or platform, good intentions can easily fade — causing opportunities to address challenges like poverty, climate change, and inequality to be missed. A dedicated platform can bring people, organizations, and advocacy groups together in one space, making it easier to connect, collaborate, and create meaningful impact.

PovertyHundreds of millions still live in extreme poverty worldwide
ClimateFloods, droughts & wildfires intensify globally each year
HungerMillions face food insecurity & malnutrition daily
EducationMany children still lack access to quality education
Gender EqualityWomen underrepresented; wage gaps persist globally
Clean WaterBillions face water scarcity for parts of the year
Affected Users

People of all ages directly or indirectly affected by global social, economic, and environmental challenges.

Core Pain Point

Many care about making a difference but lack awareness, accessible resources, or opportunities to contribute to the 17 SDGs.

Impact if Unaddressed

Without greater public engagement, efforts to achieve the SDGs are slowed, allowing global challenges to persist and affect communities.

SDG Alignment

All SDGs
Alignment Explanation

Ambag+ is aligned with all 17 Sustainable Development Goals by providing a centralized platform that increases awareness, encourages participation, and connects individuals, organizations, and communities with opportunities to contribute to sustainable development. Rather than addressing a single issue, Ambag+ empowers users to learn about, engage with, and support causes related to all SDGs — fostering collective action toward social, economic, and environmental progress.

Problem

Lack of awareness & contributions toward achieving all 17 SDGs

Website Feature

Centralized community — only SDG-related content approved

User Benefit

A platform where even the smallest contribution is celebrated

SDG Contribution

Bringing light and action to all 17 SDGs simultaneously

§ 02 cont.

Website Overview

With Ambag+, we're not just solving problems — we're building a community. One Mission, One Platform, Real Impact.
Website Description

Ambag+ is a scalable digital platform designed to increase citizen participation in the United Nations SDGs through simple, everyday actions. The system transforms small individual contributions into measurable, collective impact by integrating social interaction, gamification, and community-based competition.

Value Proposition

For students, advocacy groups, organizations, and socially conscious individuals, Ambag+ provides an engaging way to contribute to the SDGs by offering a centralized platform for completing SDG-related activities, connecting with communities, tracking impact, and earning rewards through gamified participation.

Key Features

Gamified Reward System

Rewards users with Contribution Points upon posting and interacting on the platform.

User Benefit

Sustained engagement and motivation to keep contributing to SDG causes.

AI Content Moderation

Powered by Anthropic Claude Haiku 4.5 — checks posts for profanities, hate speech, and SDG relevancy before publishing.

User Benefit

A safe, focused, and trustworthy community space for advocacy.

AI User Behavior Analysis

Monitors interactions and flags users for review or future banning if patterns indicate harmful behavior.

User Benefit

Automated enforcement keeps the platform healthy and respectful.

Streak System

Daily contributions maintain and level up the user's streak, encouraging consistent participation.

User Benefit

Builds a daily habit of advocacy and sustainable engagement.

Badges & Perks System

Users earn various badges and unlock perks as they accumulate more Contribution Points.

User Benefit

Recognition and rewards that make contribution feel meaningful.

AI Topic Suggestions

AI suggests and auto-writes topics based on trending SDG discussions, reducing friction for new users.

User Benefit

Anyone can post meaningful content — even without knowing where to start.

Bonus Feature — Campus Challenge System

Students from different National University branches compete in global challenges to earn the most Contribution Points, fostering inter-campus collaboration and healthy competition in SDG advocacy.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge

Defining a unique value proposition among existing advocacy and social platforms.

How We Solved It

Researched similar platforms and focused on gamification, contribution tracking, and SDG engagement as key differentiators.

Lesson Learned

A clear and unique value proposition is essential before development begins.

Challenge

Designing a system that supports all 17 SDGs without making the platform overly complex.

How We Solved It

Created flexible categories, campaigns, and activities mappable to any SDG.

Lesson Learned

Scalability and flexibility should be built into system architecture from the start.

Challenge

Keeping users engaged beyond their initial sign-up.

How We Solved It

Implemented leaderboards, contribution points, achievements, and community interaction features.

Lesson Learned

User retention is just as important as user acquisition.

Challenge

Balancing feature richness with a clean and user-friendly interface.

How We Solved It

Prioritized core features and refined the UI through iterative design and feedback.

Lesson Learned

Simplicity improves usability and user adoption.

Challenge

Ensuring the platform could support future growth beyond a university setting.

How We Solved It

Designed the database and user management system with scalability in mind from the start.

Lesson Learned

Thinking about future expansion early reduces redevelopment costs later.

Closing

Impact Statement

Practical Impact

Ambag+ enables students, organizations, and everyday individuals to take meaningful, consistent action on SDG-related issues through an engaging and accessible digital platform.

SDG Contribution

By centralizing awareness, participation, and advocacy for all 17 SDGs, Ambag+ amplifies collective action toward sustainable social, economic, and environmental progress.

Future Potential

Ambag+ is designed to scale from a university platform to a national and worldwide advocacy network — built for future expansion and deeper SDG integration.

Every contribution, no matter how small, has the power to create lasting impact. Ambag+ provides a platform where individuals can learn, connect, and work together toward a more sustainable and equitable future.
